MUMBAI

Mumbai, also known as Bombay, is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is the most populous city in India and the ninth most populous agglomeration in the world, with an estimated city population of 18.4 million.Mumbai lies on the west coast of India and has a deep natural harbour. In 2008, Mumbai was named an alpha world city. It is also the wealthiest city in India.

Waterkingdom

Water Kingdom is Asia's largest theme based water park. The Island of Fun is located on the coast of Mumbai, a scenic setting injected with thrill & entertainment.It is a family-friendly water park featuring high-speed slides, wave pools, lazy river & kiddie attractions. Water Kingdom delivers unlimited fun with various attractions for kids as well as for senior citizens. Experience the ultimate thrill with “Wetlantic” and get drenched at “Bratzone”- the rain dance zone!

Imagica

Adlabs Imagica is a 300-acre theme park in Khopoli, India. It is owned by Adlabs Entertainment Ltd. The park has an estimated daily capacity of 15,000 visitors.To date, the park has hosted over 3.5 million visitors They have different rides for different age groups. Guests can bump into the in-house characters of Tubbby – The Elephant, Roberto – The Star Chef, The Lost Astronaut, Mogambo of Mr. India fame and The Gingerbread Man while hopping from one ride to another.

Essel World

EsselWorld is an amusement park located in Gorai, Mumbai and established in 1989.EsselWorld is home to a variety of rides appropriate for individuals of all ages. It boasts of fourteen family rides, eleven adrenaline-pumping thrill rides and fifteen children's rides. Apart from the conventional features of a theme park such as rollercoasters and rides, EsselWorld is also host to Mumbai's first ice skating rink, spanning 3400 square feet.

Tikuji-ni-wadi

Tikuji-Ni-Wadi is an amusement park, water park, and a resort near Mumbai and at Thane. It is located at about 15 kilometres (9.3 mi) from Mumbai.The Water Park has pools and water slides. The amusement park includes go-karts, roller coasters, giant wheels and water park.[3] Additionally, there is a Shiva temple and marriage halls as a part of the resort. During Shivaratri, they make Shiva replica out of ice.The park remains open during the rainy season.
